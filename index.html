Mr. Rodwill

Date: 6/17/1856

"Everyone quiet!" He said in the corner of the room. Our teacher Mr.Rodwill was furious. He has never been like this. He's always like a flower. The next day Mr.Rodwill was the same and so he was the next day and the next day after that. Something had to be going on. I asked my friend what was going on and she said that it had to be some kind of joke. I didn't think it was a joke tho. I thought Mr.Rodwill was having some trouble with his family. I went up to him and as I was a force poushed me back into my seat. I hit my head on my desk. Everything went black.I was sent to the hospital. They were no help. Thay said that I did NOT need ANY help. Atleast I am in heaven. And Jesus I am talking to him right now. THE END!!!
